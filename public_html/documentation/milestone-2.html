Content Strategy


My site will be one page and will have the following sections:


Welcome

  • I will make this section with my name and current role which will be on top of a background image.

About Me

  • This section will detail my professional work history, work ethics, and my skills. This section will also include hobbies, explain my career goals and impact I would like to contribute to the tech industry to better give the audience a broader overview of my personality.

Portfolio:

  • I want this to be 4-5 sets of responsive images containing examples of my work. I would like a scroll over pop up window that will further include the skills, highlights and contributions to each project.

Contact:

  • This section will have a contact form for the viewer to reach out to me with any questions or concerns directly on the page. It will also have my professional email address and contact info with additional icons to my professional social media profiles: LinkedIn, GitHub, Twitter.
